Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upgrade to Premium?

You can upgrade to Premium by tapping the "Upgrade to Premium" button in the Settings menu. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete your purchase.

How do I restore my Premium purchase?

If you've previously purchased Premium, you can restore your purchase by tapping "Restore Purchases" in the Settings menu. This will restore your Premium access on any device using the same Apple ID.

What features are included in the free version?

The free version includes basic major and minor chords, standard tuning, and essential learning features. Premium features include advanced chord variations, alternative tunings, and more.

How do I report a bug or issue?

If you encounter any issues, please contact us through the email below. Include details about the problem and your device information for faster assistance.
